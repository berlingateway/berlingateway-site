Berlin Gateway Framework

A structured Qualification & Access Model designed to move candidates from eligibility assessment to a clear recognition + language + placement path.

1) Initial Eligibility Review
  • Profile snapshot (country, degree, field, experience)
  • Target route selection (recognition / visa / training / job-search)
  • Document readiness check
2) Qualification Mapping
  • Recognition logic (Anerkennung) — where applicable
  • Correct category and next steps, based on the case
  • Risk flags and bottlenecks (early detection)
3) German Language Strategy
  • Language milestones aligned with route
  • Practical exam plan (if needed)
  • Timeboxing: what to do first, what to postpone
4) Recognition & Placement Path
  • Execution plan with milestones
  • Work/education integration roadmap
  • Follow-up checkpoints
