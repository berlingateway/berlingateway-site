Your pathway to Germany

Your route depends on your qualification

In Egypt it's called vocational nursing training (تدريب مهني) — and Germany recognizes it on a par with a university degree in most cases.

Pathway 1

Vocational Nursing Diploma

  • ✓ 3-year nursing diploma from a health institute
  • ✓ Direct recognition in Germany possible
  • ✓ May require a short bridging course (3–6 months)
  • ✓ German language B2 is mandatory
  • ✓ Fastest route to employment
Pathway 2

Bachelor's in Nursing

  • ✓ Faster and easier recognition process
  • ✓ Greater opportunities for specialization
  • ✓ Higher starting salary
  • ✓ Access to supervisory and leadership roles
  • ✓ German language B2 is mandatory

Step by step

From Egypt to your first day at work in Germany

    Send your file to Berlin Gateway

    We assess your qualification, experience and language level — free of charge — and identify the best pathway for your specific situation.

  • 2

    Recognition of your qualification — Anerkennung

    We apply on your behalf to the relevant German authority to have your Egyptian nursing diploma or degree officially recognized.

    We handle the paperwork — no bureaucratic headaches

    German Language — Deutsch B2

    B2 is a legal requirement for all nurses working in Germany. The sooner you start, the faster you arrive. We recommend Goethe Institut branches in Cairo, Alexandria and other Egyptian cities.

    Average time to reach B2: 12–18 months
  • 4

    Job contract & work visa

    Berlin Gateway connects you with German hospitals and clinics that match your specialty. Once you have a contract, we prepare your visa application for the German Embassy in Cairo.

  • 5

    Arrive & settle in Germany

    Our Berlin team supports you with your first steps: city registration, bank account, health insurance — everything you need to start with confidence.
