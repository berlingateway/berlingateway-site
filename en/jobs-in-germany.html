Process

5 Steps to Work in Germany

Follow this roadmap to start your career in Germany.

  • 1

    Check Your Qualification

    Verify that your degree is recognized in Germany. Most EU/international degrees are accepted. Some professions require formal recognition.

  • 2

    Prepare Language Skills

    German B1/B2 or English. Many tech companies accept English. Healthcare and trades typically require German B1 or higher.

  • 3

    Apply for Visa

    Apply for Chancenkarte (job search) or work visa based on your profile. Processing takes 2-4 weeks. We can help with your assessment.

  • 4

    Search for Jobs

    Use LinkedIn, Indeed, StepStone, or German job boards. Network with professionals. Many jobs are filled through referrals.

  • 5

    Relocate & Settle

    Once hired, arrange accommodation and register with local authorities. Open a bank account and get a tax ID. Welcome to Germany!
