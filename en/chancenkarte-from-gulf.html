Your Options

Two main paths from the Gulf to Germany

🎯 Chancenkarte (Points-based)

For professionals without a job offer yet. You earn points based on qualification, experience, language, and age. Minimum 6 points required.

Best for: Explorers who want to job search from inside Germany

💼 Skilled Worker Visa (Direct)

If you already have a job offer from a German employer. Faster process. Recognition of your qualification may be required depending on your profession.

Best for: Those with existing employer contacts in Germany

Process

Step-by-step: Gulf to Germany

The process is the same whether you're in Dubai, Riyadh, or Doha.

  • 1

    Check your Chancenkarte points

    Use our free calculator. You need at least 6 points. Your Gulf work experience counts — even if earned outside Germany.

  • 2

    Get your qualification assessed

    For regulated professions (doctors, engineers, nurses), formal recognition is required. For IT and many others, experience is sufficient. We help you determine which applies.

  • 3

    Prepare your documents

    Degree certificates (attested and translated), passport, language certificate (B1 minimum), CV in German format, proof of finances (€5,000+ recommended).

    German embassies in UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia process Chancenkarte applications. Processing time: 4–8 weeks. Book early — appointments fill up fast.

  • 5

    Arrive and start your job search

    Register your address within 14 days. Open a German bank account. Start applying — with your Gulf experience you're a strong candidate in the German market.

FAQ

Common questions from Gulf residents

Can I apply for the Chancenkarte while living in the Gulf?

Yes — you apply at the German embassy in your current country of residence. You do not need to be in Germany or have a job offer first.

Does my Gulf work experience count toward Chancenkarte points?

Yes. Work experience earned anywhere in the world counts. If you have 3+ years of relevant experience, this adds points to your application.

I am Indian/Pakistani — is my degree recognized in Germany?

Yes, but it must be assessed. Most Indian and Pakistani degrees from HEC/UGC recognized universities are accepted. We help you check the Anabin database for your specific university.

How much money do I need to come to Germany?

The German authorities require proof of financial means. We recommend having €5,000–€8,000 available for the first months — covering deposit, living costs, and administrative fees.

Can I bring my family from the Gulf to Germany?

Yes. Once you have a stable income and residence permit, you can apply for family reunification. Processing typically takes 3–6 months.

My German is limited — can I still apply?

B1 German is the minimum for the Chancenkarte language points. However, you can apply without German if you score enough points through other criteria. Many IT roles in Germany also work in English.
