Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs About Working in Germany

What language do I need to work in Germany?

German B1/B2 is ideal, but many international companies accept English. It's recommended to learn German while job searching.

How long does the Chancenkarte process take?

Application typically takes 2-4 weeks. The Chancenkarte then allows 12 months of residence to search for a job.

Can I work with the Chancenkarte?

Yes, limited employment is possible while job searching. Once you have a job contract, you can apply for a skilled worker visa.

What are the cost of living in Germany?

Cost of living varies by city. In Berlin and other major cities, expect between €1,200 and €2,000 per month.
