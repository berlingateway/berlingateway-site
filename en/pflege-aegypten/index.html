Your pathway to Germany

Your route depends on your qualification

In Egypt it's called vocational nursing training (تدريب مهني) — and Germany recognizes it on a par with a university degree in most cases.

Pathway 1

Vocational Nursing Diploma

Technical institutes or 5-year nursing schools.

  • ✓ 3–5 years of study
  • ✓ Experience in MOH or University hospitals
  • ✓ Curriculum evaluation for equivalence
  • ✓ Direct recognition or adaptation period
  • ✓ German B2 level required
Pathway 2

Bachelor of Nursing (BScN)

Faculty of Nursing graduates.

  • ✓ University degree (4 years + internship)
  • ✓ Completion of the internship year
  • ✓ Professional practice license
  • ✓ Full recognition (Anerkennung) process
  • ✓ Berlin Gateway support for your file
